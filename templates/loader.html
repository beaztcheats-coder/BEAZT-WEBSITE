Setup

Loader Guide

Follow the steps below to get your loader running. Pre-requirements must be installed first.

1

Get Your Loader

Loaders are attached to each product. After purchasing, download the loader for your product from the My Keys page — it appears on your key card.

Go to My Keys {% if not current_user.is_authenticated %} Sign in {% endif %}
2

Download RivaTuner Statistics Server

Required for the overlay to function correctly. Install the latest version from the official mirror.

Download RivaTuner
3

Disable Antivirus Real-Time Protection

Windows Defender and most antivirus software will flag game cheats as false positives. Temporarily disable real-time protection before downloading and running the loader.

Windows Defender: Settings → Privacy & Security → Virus & Threat Protection → Manage Settings → Turn off Real-time Protection
4

Enter Your License Key

After purchasing a license, find your key on the My Keys page. Copy it and paste it into the loader to activate.
