Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register or purchase, we collect:

  • Email address and username (provided during registration)
  • Payment metadata processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
  • License key usage data for service delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and deliver license keys
  • Provide customer support via Discord
  • Send service-related notifications (updates, maintenance)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Payment Processing

Stripe processes all payments via encrypted checkout. We do not store full card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

4. Data Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption, salted password hashing, and routine security reviews.

5. Cookies

We use essential session cookies for login and Stripe fraud-prevention cookies at checkout. We do not use ad-tracking cookies.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Google Fonts — website typography (loaded from Google CDN)
  • Discord — customer support platform

7. Data Retention

We retain account and order data while your account is active. Key-related data is kept for the license period plus 30 days. You can request deletion via Discord.

8. Your Rights

Based on your region, you may have GDPR/CCPA rights to access, correct, delete, or export data. Contact us via Discord to request this.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy over time. Major changes are announced in Discord. Continued use means acceptance of the update.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us via Discord. Our support team is available 24/7.
